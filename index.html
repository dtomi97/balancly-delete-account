Delete Account

Balancly – Finance Manager  ·  Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your Balancly account and all associated data directly from within the app at any time. No need to contact us — the deletion is immediate and permanent.

How to delete your account

1
Open the Balancly app and make sure you are signed in.
2
Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
3
Scroll down to the Delete Account section.
4
Tap Delete Account and confirm when prompted.
5
Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.
⚠️ This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered. Make sure to export your data first if you wish to keep a copy (Profile → Data Management → Export).

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase account and login credentials
  • All transactions — expenses and incomes
  • All custom categories and category limits
  • All recurring expenses
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Your RevenueCat subscription record (GDPR erasure)

Active subscriptions are not automatically cancelled when you delete your account. Please cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store before deleting your account to avoid further charges.

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to sign in to the app and need your account deleted, please contact us directly and we will process the deletion manually within 30 days:

balancly.support@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Balancly account in your request.

Data retention after deletion

All personal data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. Backup copies stored in Firebase's infrastructure may persist for up to 30 days before being fully purged. No personal data is retained beyond this period.
